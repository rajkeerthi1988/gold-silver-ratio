What is the Gold-Silver Ratio?

The Gold-Silver Ratio measures how many ounces of silver are required to buy one ounce of gold. Investors track this ratio to identify potential relative value between the two precious metals.

Historically, a high ratio may indicate that silver is undervalued relative to gold, while a low ratio may suggest that gold is undervalued compared to silver.
